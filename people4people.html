What is People 4 People?

Our mission is to create a supportive space where people with disabilities can learn to advocate for themselves and use their voices to push for equal opportunity. In our group, we help you contact government officials, write letters, make phone calls, and participate in special events—such as traveling to the State Capitol in Montgomery to meet with legislators—or simply figure out how to stand up for your rights in everyday life.

Whether you’re newly injured or years into your journey, you’ll find encouragement, accountability, and a community that is understanding of your disability. To learn more, please see our Contact page or talk with our coordinator, Brian Johnson, at the next meeting.
